Welcome to CampusBite! This guide will help you navigate the platform and order food from your favorite campus outlets.

## Browse campus stores

When you log in, you'll see all available food outlets on campus.

<Steps>
  <Step title="View available stores">
    The home page displays all active campus food outlets with their operating hours and images. You can search for specific stores using the search bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check active orders">
    If you have any active orders, you'll see a banner at the top showing how many orders are in progress. Click "View" to track them.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select a store">
    Click on any store card or the "View Menu" button to see what they offer.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Stores display their operating hours as badges. Make sure to order during their active hours.
</Note>

## Order food

Once you've found what you want to eat, adding items to your cart is simple.

### Browse the menu

- **Search items**: Use the search bar to find specific dishes quickly
- **Filter by category**: If the store has categories (like "Main Course", "Beverages", "Snacks"), use the category tabs to filter
- **Check availability**: Unavailable items are marked with an "Unavailable" badge

### Add items to cart

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add your first item">
    Click the "Add to Cart" button on any available item. You'll see a success message confirming the item was added.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust quantities">
    Once an item is in your cart, you'll see +/- buttons to adjust the quantity. The cart updates automatically.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View cart summary">
    A floating cart bar appears at the bottom showing your item count and total amount. Click "View Cart" when you're ready.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can only order from one store at a time. If you try adding items from a different store, you'll be asked to replace your current cart.
</Tip>

### Review your cart

On the cart page, you can:

- **Adjust quantities** or remove items using the controls
- **Add special instructions** (optional) for the store, like "extra spicy" or "no onions"
- **See your total** before proceeding to checkout

## Complete your order

### Checkout process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start checkout">
    Click "Proceed to Checkout" from your cart to begin the payment process.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Initiate payment">
    Review your order summary and click "Continue to Payment" to generate your payment details.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pay via UPI">
    You'll receive:
    - Store UPI ID to pay to
    - A unique payment reference code
    - QR code for easy scanning
    
    Pay the exact amount shown using any UPI app.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit payment confirmation">
    After paying, click "I Paid Successfully". You can optionally enter your UPI transaction ID for faster verification.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The payment reference code helps the store identify your payment. It's automatically included when you scan the QR code.
</Note>

### Trust tier system

CampusBite uses a trust tier system to ensure reliability:

- **Good standing**: Order normally without restrictions
- **Watch tier**: You may need to confirm you're on the way before the store starts preparing
- **Restricted**: Temporary ordering restriction after repeated no-shows

<Tip>
  Always pick up your orders on time to maintain good standing. If your status is "watch" or lower, confirm your commitment quickly when prompted.
</Tip>

## Track your order

After submitting your order, you can track its progress in real-time.

### Order statuses

Your order goes through these stages:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Order Placed" icon="shopping-bag">
    Your order is submitted and waiting for the store to verify payment and accept it.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Accepted" icon="check-circle">
    The store confirmed your payment and accepted your order.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Preparing" icon="utensils">
    Your food is being prepared right now.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Ready for Pickup" icon="bell">
    Your order is ready! Head to the store to collect it.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Pickup process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Receive pickup notification">
    When your order is ready, you'll receive a notification and see a 6-digit OTP on the order tracking page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Go to the store">
    Head to the food outlet within the pickup timeframe.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Show your OTP">
    Show or tell the store staff your 6-digit OTP to collect your order.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Order completed">
    Once verified, your order is marked as picked up. Enjoy your meal!
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  The OTP expires after a certain time. Make sure to pick up your order promptly after receiving the ready notification.
</Note>

## Commitment confirmation

If you're in the "watch" trust tier or have previous no-shows, you may need to confirm your commitment:

- You'll see a prompt to confirm "I Am On The Way" after placing your order
- This helps stores avoid preparing food that won't be picked up
- Confirm within the deadline shown to proceed with your order
- Failure to confirm may result in automatic cancellation

## View order history

Access your past orders from the "Order History" page:

- See all your previous orders with dates and amounts
- Track the status of active orders
- View order details including items, payment info, and special instructions

<Tip>
  Keep track of your order history to reorder your favorite meals quickly.
</Tip>
